Message from the Bishopric

D&C 6:36 "Look unto me in every thought; doubt not, fear not." This is our youth theme this year. Many of our youth have participated in FSY Conferences, Stake Girls Camp, and Stake Aaronic Priesthood Camp over the past month. I've been priviledged to participate in several of these events and have felt our youth drawing closer to Christ as they focus on Him. I invite you to visit with our youth and have them share their experiences and testimonies of the Savior. I know we each will be blessed as we each individually seek to increase our faith in the Savior Jesus Christ.

- Bishop Hanks
