Message from the Bishopric

President Russell M. Nelson taught “Counting our blessings is far better than recounting our problems. No matter our situation, showing gratitude for our privileges is a unique, fast-acting, and long-lasting spiritual prescription.” Recently I have been participating in the Finding Strength in the Lord: Emotional Resilience class and have been more fully taught and appreciative of the true power of being more grateful in my life. I invite you to write down one thing you are grateful for each day for the month of November and share it with someone else. I know you too will more fully feel the influence of the Savior Jesus Christ in your life as you do.

- Bishop Hanks
